Kare-kare recipe

On this page we will show you how to cook your favorite dish.

discover watch



Sauce
Ingredients Quantity
Simpol Crispy Pork Belly 4-6 servings
garlic, minced 1 head
onion, chopped 2 pieces
annatto seeds ½ cup
flour ½ cup
peanut butter ½ cup
beef stock 4-5 cups
Oil as needed
Salt and pepper to taste
Vegetables
Ingredients Quantity
banana heart 1 piece
sitaw 12 pieces
eggplant 2 pieces
native pechay 1 bunch



Procedure

  1. prepare annatto oil
  2. saute garlic and onion
  3. add flour
  4. add beef stock and peanut butter
  5. blanch vegetables
  6. serve veggies on meat on top of sauce
